For the empowerment of girls and women. Participants are volunteers selected by the villagers and approved for the readiness for service.

The participants for the village health promotion program are volunteers selected by the villagers and approved for the readiness for service.

The quality of health services in the villages is monitored by the Nurses of the dispensary in whose area the village falls. The feedback comes through the VHP’s organizing village health committees and reporting every month to the dispensary statt.

As tribal society is very stable there are few risks visible. The non-availability of trained VHP’s because of marriage, sickness or alternative employment are the possible reasons for decline. The remedy is to appoint and train new volunteers.
